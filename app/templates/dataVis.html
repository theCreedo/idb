Data Visualization

Magic the Gathering's Subtypes

This data was visualized by collecting every single card from Magic the Gathering. Although every card is unique, every single card belongs to a certain subtype group. Every occurrence of a subtype was counted and displayed in a large bubble cloud sized according to their count.
